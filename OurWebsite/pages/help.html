Help & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

How do I update my personal information?

You can update your personal information through the "My Account" page. Simply log in, navigate to your account page, and update your details.

What should I do if I have a billing issue?

If you encounter a billing issue, please reach out to our support team via the "Contact Us" section. We will assist you in resolving any payment-related issues.

How can I cancel my appointments?

To cancel an appointment, visit your "Appointments" page, select the appointment you wish to cancel, and click the "Cancel Appointment" button.

Who can I contact for support?

For further assistance, you can contact our customer support team via the "Help" section or send us an email at support@pillpilot.com.
